Our team combines years of knowledge from formerly building combustion race cars and building electric race cars. Our years of mechanical development let us tackle these new technologies head-on while maintaining a solid racing platform. We strive to push Concordia engineering students to become top-notch, relevant future employees in an ever-changing job market. Here at Concordia Formula Racing, we don't just make race cars go fast, we create the race car of the future.
